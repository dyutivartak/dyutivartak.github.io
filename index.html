About Me

I'm an adaptable Data Science & Software Engineering professional with a Master's in Data Science from the University of Washington and a 9.3/10 CGPA B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science) from the University of Mumbai. I thrive at the intersection of analytics, machine learning, and product-driven engineering.

I specialize in designing end-to-end ML pipelines, deploying models into production, and leveraging Python, R, and SQL to solve real-world problems with experience in domains such as global trade, food security, and fraud detection.

My passion? Turning raw data into reliable, actionable insight and then embedding that intelligence into production-ready systems. Whether it's predicting crop yields using causal inference, analyzing freezing-of-gait in Parkinson's disease, building user-facing ML applications, or architecting Streamlit-based web tools, my goal remains the same: deliver measurable impact by making complex problems tangible.

I solve three core problems for organizations: Insight gap, Model-to-product gap, and User adoption gap. If you're looking for someone who can drive analytics and build scalable data-driven systems, then let's connect. I'm actively seeking full-time roles where I can bring my blend of data science, software engineering, and product mindset to your next big challenge.
